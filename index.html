Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions businesses usually ask before booking a demo.

What file types does Zendix support?

Zendix supports PDFs, PNG, JPEG/JPG, WEBP, and TXT files natively within standard parsing sequences.

Can teams review extracted data before exporting it?

Yes. Teams can compare extracted data with the source file, edit values, and approve results. Teams can only export extracted data once approved.

Can Zendix handle different departments or customers?

Yes. Multi-environment workflows let businesses completely separate documents by department, client, or specific extraction workflows.

Does Zendix support email automation?

Yes. It can automatically process incoming business emails and attachments completely on demand with automated cron listeners.

Can extracted data be exported?

Yes. Clean structured data can be exported to CSV or Excel for instant manual mapping into other enterprise applications.

How does Zendix reduce errors?

Zendix pairs precision multi-modal extraction with mandatory human review workflows and dynamic fail reason detection alerts.
